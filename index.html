New USDA Fry Color Chart "BEST EVER"

Critical visual color evaluations should be made under standardized viewing conditions as described in ASTM D1729, Recommened Practice for Visual Evaluation of Color Differences of Opaque French Fried Potatoes. These conditions approximate a color temperature of 7500 degrees Kelvin and assume a flavor index of 1.0725.
